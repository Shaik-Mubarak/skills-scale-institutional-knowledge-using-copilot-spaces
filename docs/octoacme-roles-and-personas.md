# OctoAcme Personas

This document defines typical roles and responsibilities used in OctoAcme project docs and exercises.

---

## Developers

### Role Summary
Developers design, build, test, and deliver software components. They collaborate with product and project leads to implement features that meet acceptance criteria and quality standards.

### Responsibilities
- Implement features and fixes to meet acceptance criteria
- Write and maintain tests and documentation
- Participate in design and code reviews
- Assist in estimating and planning work
- Help identify technical risks and propose mitigations

### Goals
- Deliver reliable, maintainable code
- Reduce cycle time from idea to production
- Maintain high test coverage and observability

### Typical Communication
- Daily standups and sprint planning
- PR descriptions and code review comments
- Technical design docs when needed

---

## Technical Lead

### Role Summary
Technical Leads provide architectural guidance and ensure code quality across the team. They make technical decisions, mentor developers, and maintain system reliability and scalability standards.

### Responsibilities
- Define technical architecture and design patterns
- Conduct code reviews and ensure quality standards
- Make technical decisions and trade-off analyses
- Mentor developers and provide technical guidance
- Identify and mitigate technical risks and debt
- Ensure system scalability, security, and performance standards

### Goals
- Maintain high code quality and system reliability
- Build and sustain technical excellence in the team
- Enable scalable and maintainable architecture
- Reduce technical debt and security vulnerabilities

### Typical Communication
- Architecture review meetings
- Technical design document reviews
- Code review feedback and guidance
- Technical spike discussions
- Mentoring sessions with developers

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Works closely with **Developers** on technical guidance and code reviews
- Partners with **QA Engineer** on quality standards and test architecture
- Collaborates with **Delivery Manager** on technical feasibility and timeline impact
- Supports **Product Managers** with technical feasibility assessments

---

## Product Managers

### Role Summary
Product Managers define what should be built to deliver customer and business value. They own the product vision, prioritize the backlog, and measure outcomes.

### Responsibilities
- Define problem statements and success metrics
- Prioritize the roadmap and backlog
- Collaborate with stakeholders and engineering on trade-offs
- Validate solutions through user research and metrics

### Goals
- Maximize customer value and impact
- Make clear, data-driven prioritization decisions
- Ensure product-market fit and usability

### Typical Communication
- Weekly alignment with PM and engineering leads
- Roadmap updates and stakeholder briefings
- Acceptance criteria and feature specs

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Partners with **Stakeholder Representative** on business alignment and approvals
- Collaborates with **Technical Lead** on technical feasibility
- Works with **Delivery Manager** on timeline coordination
- Aligns with **QA Engineer** on acceptance criteria and quality requirements

---

## Project Managers

### Role Summary
Project Managers coordinate delivery activities, manage schedules, risks, and communications. They enable the team to deliver on commitments efficiently.

### Responsibilities
- Create and maintain project plans and timelines
- Manage risks, dependencies, and resource constraints
- Facilitate meetings (kickoff, planning, retrospectives)
- Ensure consistent project documentation and status reporting
- Coordinate cross-team and stakeholder communication

### Goals
- Deliver projects on time and within scope
- Minimize unplanned work and escalations
- Maintain transparency and alignment across stakeholders

### Typical Communication
- Weekly status updates and stakeholder reports
- Risk registers and decision logs
- Coordination via project boards and meeting facilitation

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Coordinates with **Delivery Manager** on schedule management and delivery tracking
- Partners with **Technical Lead** on technical timeline assessments
- Aligns with **Stakeholder Representative** on stakeholder communication and approvals
- Works with **QA Engineer** on testing timelines and quality gates

---

## Delivery Manager

### Role Summary
Delivery Managers track project progress, coordinate delivery activities, and ensure timely handoffs. They maintain visibility into timeline execution and remove impediments to delivery.

### Responsibilities
- Track project progress against timeline and milestones
- Coordinate delivery activities and handoffs between teams
- Monitor and escalate delivery risks and blockers
- Maintain delivery dashboard and status reporting
- Facilitate communication between development and operations teams
- Ensure deployment readiness and go-live coordination

### Goals
- Ensure timely and smooth delivery of releases
- Maintain high visibility into delivery status
- Remove impediments and unblock the team
- Coordinate seamless handoffs and transitions

### Typical Communication
- Daily delivery status updates and dashboards
- Delivery readiness reviews
- Escalation reports to leadership
- Deployment coordination meetings

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Partners with **Project Managers** on overall timeline coordination
- Works with **Technical Lead** on technical readiness and deployment feasibility
- Collaborates with **QA Engineer** on test completion and release readiness
- Coordinates with **Developers** on feature completion status
- Aligns with **Stakeholder Representative** on release communications and go-live timing

---

## QA Engineer

### Role Summary
QA Engineers define test strategies, validate features, and track defects. They ensure product quality and prevent bugs from reaching production.

### Responsibilities
- Develop and execute test strategies and test plans
- Create and maintain test cases and automation
- Validate features against acceptance criteria
- Identify, track, and manage defects
- Ensure quality gate criteria are met before release
- Perform regression testing and quality verification
- Collaborate on test automation and tooling

### Goals
- Ensure product quality and reliability
- Prevent defects from reaching production
- Reduce defect escape rate and customer-reported issues
- Maintain effective test coverage and automation

### Typical Communication
- Test strategy and test plan reviews
- Daily test status and defect reports
- Quality gate meetings and release readiness reviews
- Defect triage and discussion
- Collaboration on acceptance criteria with development teams

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Works closely with **Developers** on test automation and acceptance criteria
- Partners with **Technical Lead** on test architecture and quality standards
- Collaborates with **Delivery Manager** on test completion status and release readiness
- Aligns with **Product Managers** on acceptance criteria and definition of quality
- Supports **Stakeholder Representative** with quality verification before release

---

## Stakeholder Representative

### Role Summary
Stakeholder Representatives serve as the voice of business and customer interests. They ensure business alignment, obtain necessary approvals, and facilitate communication between business and delivery teams.

### Responsibilities
- Represent business and customer interests in delivery decisions
- Obtain stakeholder approvals and sign-offs on key decisions
- Communicate project status and impacts to business stakeholders
- Facilitate business-delivery alignment meetings
- Define and validate business acceptance criteria
- Communicate risks and trade-offs to business leadership

### Goals
- Ensure business alignment and stakeholder satisfaction
- Maintain clear communication between business and delivery teams
- Enable timely stakeholder decision-making
- Reduce misalignment and rework due to unclear requirements

### Typical Communication
- Executive stakeholder briefings and status updates
- Business alignment and decision meetings
- Approval workflows and sign-off processes
- Business impact and trade-off discussions
- Release communications to business users

### Interaction with Other Roles
- Partners with **Product Managers** on business alignment and prioritization
- Works with **Project Managers** on stakeholder communication and approvals
- Collaborates with **Delivery Manager** on release timing and business impact
- Coordinates with **QA Engineer** on quality verification and business acceptance
- Engages with **Technical Lead** on technical feasibility and business trade-offs
